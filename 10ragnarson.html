Challenges

The main goal was to create the engaging event website for Ragnarson 10th anniversary. The concept was to present company history in a brief, pleasant way and don't bore the user.

Solutions

The layout consists of full-screen cards - each card contains a short information about the crucial event for the company. They are ordered chronologically so that the user is brought for a quick journey through the history. Cards have their own unique colors and graphics. To make the website more interactive and fun, I decided to add animations based on a scroll position.
