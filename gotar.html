Challenges

Design simple, intuitive & informative website for a small company, as well as the logo and business cards.

Solutions

The company name is associated with a dwarf - that's why the logo presents dwarf's face, drawn in a minimal, geometric way. To add the visual connection with the IT industry I've used 3d glitch effect. Both the business cards & website are visually based on logo appearance. Website structure is divided into sections with all necessary information such as work, about information, contact etc. All of these are placed in the intuitive navigation at the top of the page. The homepage is a brief sneak peek of all the information that we can find on the website. The important aspects here are the details - according to the clean & simple layout we can find here slight animations that give the breeze of fresh air. I took care of RWD as well so that the website looks good also on mobile devices.

Tools used

Affinity Designer / Elixir / Phoenix / slim / sass / hocus-pocus CSS Framework / GIT
